Froot Loops

Vitals

  • Servings: 1
  • Cook time: none
  • Prep time: 2-3 minutes

    • Ingredients

    • 1 Box of Kellog's Froot Loops Cereal
    • 1 cup of milk of choice
    • 1 spoon of preferred size
    • 1 bowl of preferred size

    Preparation

    • 1. Pour desired amount of cereal into bowl
    • 2. Pour desired amount of milk into bowl on top of cereal
    • 3. Close up cereal box and place in pantry
    • 4. Close up milk and place in refrigerator
    • 5. Scoop cereal with milk into the spoon
    • 6. Eat and enjoy!

S'mores

Vitals

  • Servings: 1
  • Cook time: 3-5 minutes
  • Prep time: 3-5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 marshmallow
  • 1 full size graham cracker
  • 1/2 bar of Hersheys Chocolate
  • 1 Marshmallow roasting tool
  • Heat source for marshmallow

Preparation

  • 1. Break graham cracker into two halves
  • 2. Place half bar of chocolate onto graham cracker
  • 3. Place marshmallow onto roasting tool/stick
  • 4. Heat marshmallow with heat source for desired length
  • 5. Place cooked marshmallow onto chocolate and graham cracker
  • 6. Use other half of graham cracker to sandwich marshmallow
  • 7. Eat and enjoy!

Hot Dogs

Vitals

  • Servings: 3-8
  • Cook time: 3-6 minutes
  • Prep time: 2-5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 package of 8 pre-cooked hot dogs
  • 8 hot dog buns
  • Medium size pot
  • Water
  • Condiments

Preparation

  • 1. Fill pot up halfway with water and bring to a boil
  • 2. Place hot dogs into boiling water for roughly 3-6 minutes
  • 3. When dogs are warm throughout and plump, remove from water
  • 4. Place hot dog in bun and add condiments of choice
  • 5. Eat and enjoy!
